Serena Bonaretti, PhD

I am an independent research scientist at Transparent MSK Research and an independent teacher at C as in Coding.
I am writing the book Learn Python with Jupyter.

Transparent MSK Research
I develop open and reproducible tools for image acquisition, image processing, and biomechanics to investigate aging diseases of the musculoskeletal system. See my latest release pyKNEEr.
In addition, I am leading the creation of the Open and Reproducible Musculoskeletal Imaging Research (ORMIR) community, where we develop open and reproducible workflows to analyze musculoskeletal images.
I also promote transparent research on my YouTube channel and in talks and workshops. All my material is freely available.

C as in Coding
After teaching at major worldwide universities, I currently enjoy teaching Python programming and scientific Python using Jupyter Notebook to students of any age and background.
